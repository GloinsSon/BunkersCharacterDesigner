Bunkers Character Designer

You can choose the look of your Bunker, the inhabitants of Bunkin; Land of personified stuffed animals, they dream of having a master.

  1. Pick your species
  2. Roll the dice (re-roll for dice with 15-19)
  3. Drag & drop to switch dice
  4. Choose from the available options
  5. Download your Bunker as a vector graphic (svg) to be edited with programs like Inkscape
